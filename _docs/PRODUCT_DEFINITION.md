# Product Definition Summary

# DealSniperAI

## Category

Business Acquisition Intelligence Platform.

## User Segments

- Acquisition entrepreneurs
- Business brokers
- Independent sponsors
- Family offices
- Private equity roll-up operators
- Growth consultants
- KlickSmartAI internal deal sourcing

## Core Promise

Find local businesses that are not listed for sale yet, but show signals of likely ownership transition, strong deal quality, and operational upside.

## Product Modules

### 1. Business Discovery

Collect local business records from Google Business Profile and supporting public sources.

### 2. Signal Intelligence

Map raw public data into acquisition signals.

### 3. Scoring Engine

Calculate API, DQS, VCS, and ARI.

### 4. Deal Profile

Create a structured acquisition profile for each business.

### 5. Watchlist

Track businesses over time and rescore quarterly.

### 6. Acquisition Brief

Generate a concise, evidence-backed acquisition thesis.

### 7. CRM Pipeline

Route qualified opportunities into relationship management.

## MVP Outcome

A user can upload or receive a scored business payload and get back:

- Deal scores
- Classification
- Evidence-backed signals
- Recommended action
- Stored profile

## Future Outcome

A continuously updating local-business acquisition intelligence graph.
