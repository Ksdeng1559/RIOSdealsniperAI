# RIOS Framework Context

RIOS means:

```text
Research → Intelligence → Opportunity → Strategy → Execution
```

## Research

Collect public data from Google Business Profile, websites, reviews, public registries, social pages, job posts, and technology indicators.

## Intelligence

Convert raw data into structured signals:

- Retirement signals
- Burnout signals
- Succession signals
- Technology gap signals
- Financial stress signals
- Deal quality signals
- Value creation signals

## Opportunity

Classify each business:

- A+ Acquisition Candidate
- A Strategic Opportunity
- B Watchlist
- C Low Priority

## Strategy

Determine the best path:

- Acquisition conversation
- Partnership conversation
- Fractional operator offer
- Watchlist monitoring
- No action

## Execution

Generate:

- Acquisition brief
- Owner profile
- Value creation thesis
- CRM record
- Watchlist record
- Reviewable outreach draft
